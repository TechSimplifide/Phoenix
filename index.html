FAQs

What is Phoenix Library System?

Phoenix is a modern Library Management System that helps you manage books, users, and records efficiently with a professional UI.

How can I register as a new member?

Click the "Register" button in the navigation and fill out the form. Once verified, you can borrow and reserve books.

What happens if I return a book late?

A small late fee will apply per day after the due date. You'll be notified by email and SMS reminders.

Is there a mobile view for students?

Yes, the UI is fully responsive and optimized for phones and tablets.
